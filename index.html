My Inventions

Ingenuity is the great power to be creative and obtain great power! I have used the ingenuity in my brain to make some of the best creations know to man... maybe better than sliced bread... dare I say... the wheel?!

1. Water Bottle Taco Holder

My idea for the water bottle taco holder is one of the best! It puts a simple device on the water bottle with a simple holder for your taco so the way Taco Tuesdays are now effortless!

2. Water Leash

This amazing contraption allows your child to enjoy a day at the pool without the stress of them floating away or drowning. A simple vest, leash combo lets your little one splish and splash and you can just give them a tug and wathc them cheerfully drift back!

3. Tasty Maker

This absolutely ingenious machine allows you to make the perfect cookies every single time! All you have to do is put your ingredients for the cookie you wan to bake and *poof*! The Tasy Maker delivers perfect cookie dough balls ready to bake for the tastiest cookie every single time.

4. Warm Water

Ever have your dogs water bowl freeze over during the cold winter days? Well fear no more! The Warm Water is a dog bowl that stays warm for your fuzzy friends with its double wall insulation system. Just pop in a couple batteries and your dogs water won't freeze even on the coldest of days.
